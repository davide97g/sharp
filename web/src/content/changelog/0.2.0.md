### Added
- A unified Calendar area for Google events and meetings scheduled inside sharp.
- Read-only Google Calendar connection with selectable calendars and automatic background sync.
- Scheduled meetings for channels, direct messages, standalone calls, or personal calendar entries.
- Invitations, RSVP responses, chat cards, join links, and lead-time or start-time reminders.

### Changed
- Calendar reminders use the same in-app, native, and web-push delivery pipeline as workspace notifications.
- Connected-calendar credentials are encrypted at rest and remain private to each user.
