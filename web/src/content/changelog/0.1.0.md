### Added
- Real-time channels, direct messages, threads, reactions, search, replies, emoji, and presence.
- Collaborative documents and canvases with live cursors, flexible sharing roles, backlinks, trash, and chat mentions.
- File attachments, an inbox, web push, desktop notifications, channel muting, and Do Not Disturb.
- Peer-to-peer voice and video calls with screen sharing, picture-in-picture, guest links, device controls, and hand raising.
- Searchable meeting notes generated from opt-in call transcripts, including summaries and action items.
- A GIF picker, context-aware GIF suggestions, and shared duck streaks for livelier conversations.
- Native desktop apps with secure browser sign-in, plus self-hosting options for Docker, Caddy, and Dokploy.

### Changed
- The workspace rail now keeps Chat, Docs, Canvas, Meetings, and Calendar within easy reach.
- Channel roles now support owners, editors, and viewers, with permissions inherited by documents and canvases.
- Deployment can serve the API, web app, and landing page together or as separate services.

### Fixed
- Protected member email addresses from being exposed to other workspace users.
- Improved message action placement, canvas routing, notification navigation, and call microphone state.
