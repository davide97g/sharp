---
title: Voice & video
order: 4
---

Start or join a call from a channel or direct message. Choose microphone and camera devices from the call controls. You can mute, enable video, raise your hand, or share one screen or tab.

Calls support up to eight audio participants, four cameras, and one screen share. Picture-in-picture keeps the call visible while you move around sharp. Owners and editors can create a guest link when someone outside the workspace needs to join.
