Open **Calendar** to see Google events and sharp meetings together. Connect Google from Calendar settings, choose visible calendars, and sync. Google access is read-only.

Create a sharp meeting from the agenda or drag across the week grid. Add a channel, call, or attendees as needed. Invitees can RSVP; creators can edit or cancel. Reminder notifications include a join path when one is available.
