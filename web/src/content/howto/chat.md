title: Chat
order: 1
---

Choose a channel or direct message from the sidebar. Write Markdown, add emoji or attachments, then send. Type `@` to mention a person and `#` to link a document or canvas.

Hover over a message to react, edit your own message, quote it, or open a thread. Use search to look across your channels, or scope results to one channel. Muting a channel stops its alerts without leaving it.
